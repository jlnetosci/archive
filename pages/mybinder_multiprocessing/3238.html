All the clusters backing mybinder.org should give you 1 core. I don‚Äôt know a general way to ask the question ‚Äúhow many cores does the container I am running in actually have access to?‚Äù. Asking for the number of CPUs (while inside a container) mostly just tells you the number of cores the host has :-/
